## Estonia Tax Rate Increase

Estonia has increased the standard VAT rate from 22% to 24% as of July 1, 2025.

## Romania to Follow

At the beginning of July, the Romanian parliament approved a tax rate increase from 19 to 21 percent as of August 1, 2025. The country is following a recommendation of the International Monetary Fund (IMF).

### Additional Changes in Romania

- One of the reduced tax rates will be increased from 9 to 11 percent
- This affects food, water, books, and hotel/catering services
- Under certain conditions, a VAT rate of 9% continues to apply to residential real estate until August 1, 2026

## Impact for Businesses

These changes affect VAT refund calculations for businesses operating in these countries. Ensure your systems are updated to reflect the new rates for accurate VAT recovery claims.
